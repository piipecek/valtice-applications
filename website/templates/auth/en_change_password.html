  • Your password has been created by the organizers. We strongly recommend you to choose a new one.

What does a good password look like?

  • It should be longer than 8 characters.
  • It should contain some special character other than letters or numbers.
  • It should be unique.
  • If you use a password manager, it doesn't have to be memorable by humans.
