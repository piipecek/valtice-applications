Child registration

A child must be assigned to an already existing account of a responsible person. If such an account does not exist yet, the person must go through registration as a person who is already 16 years old.

The child participant may or may not have their own email and password. If you choose to provide them with an email and password, they will be able to log in themselves. If not, the account will only be manageable from the account of the responsible person.

How does a good password look like?

  • It should be longer than 8 characters.
  • It should contain some special character other than letters or numbers.
  • It should be unique.
  • If you use a password manager, it doesn't need to be humanly memorable.
