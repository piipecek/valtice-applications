ISSEM registration - introduction

Before registration, you surely have some questions about the changes from previous years. Everything is clearly explained on this page.

Child participant registration

A Child must be assigned to an existing account of a responsible person. If such an account does not exist yet, the responsible person must go through the registration process again as a person who is already 15 years old.

A child participant may or may not have their own email and password. If you decide to enter an email and password for them, they will be able to log in themselves. If not, the account will be managed only from the account of the responsible person.

E-mail of the responsible person:

A notice that a child has been assigned will be sent to this email.

What does a good password look like?

  • It should be longer than 8 characters.
  • It should contain some special character other than letters or numbers.
  • It should be unique.
  • If you use a password manager, it doesn't have to be memorable by humans.
You can always recover your password if you forget it.
